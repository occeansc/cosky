The Edge

The RS Lifecycle

Relative Strength (RS = stock price ÷ SPY) is a leading indicator of institutional money flow. Each COSKY version enters at a different point on the RS arc and exits when that stage is complete.

5.0 Phoenix — deepest RS collapse + first recovery cross. Prior-peak exit.
4.0 Dark Horse — 48-week RS low turning up. Exits at RS 52-week high.
3.0 RS Reversal — RS crosses 20-day EMA from weakness. Exits before crowd confirms.
2.0 Regime+VCP — confirmed RS leader in volatility contraction base.
1.0 RS High — 52-week RS high. Institutional consensus confirmed.
🛡
SPY Regime Gate — Versions 2.0, 3.0, 4.0, and 5.0 halt all new long entries when SPY trades below its 20-day EMA. In 2022 (bear market), COSKY 1.0 (no gate) had the worst drawdown. All gated versions sidestepped the majority of it.
